Getting Started!
Deposit Ethereum into a long term stable solution where your Ethereum can grow organically as the system expands. You will gain +.5% of your deposited Ethereum every day that you leave it in the DailyDivs Earn Game Smart Contract. You can withdraw your deposited Ethereum at any time but it will incur a 20% withdrawal fee (~13 days of depositing). You can not withdraw ETH when you buy a position, some must buy the position from you. You can also invest your profits back into your account and your gains will compound the more you do this!
Compete for Profits - (Click to read)
For those looking for larger profits, you can compete for the investment positions available. Each time someone makes a deposit into the DailyDivs Smart Contract, you will receive a percentage of that sale which goes straight to your affiliate commision. You can buy this position off anyone and double it's current buying price however, every 3-7 days (depending on the position), the buying price will halve until it reaches 0.125 ether. Upon buying, the previous investor gets 75% of the buying price, the dev gets 5% and the rest goes into the contract to encourage an all round balanced ecosystem!
Affiliate Commissions - (Click to read)
You will also receive a 3% bonus, which will appear in your affiliate commission, by referring other players to the game via your referral URL! Our smart contract is fully transparent and you'll never need to worry about an exit scam or someone taking all the money and leaving!
